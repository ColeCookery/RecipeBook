Spaghetti Bolognese

Category: Main Dish

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© onion and garlic.
  3. Add minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve over spaghetti.
