Chicken Gyro

Ingredients

Chicken Breast
Greek Pita Bread
Romaine Lettuce
Tomato
Red Onion
French Fries (Optional)
Tzatziki
	Plain Greek Yogurt
	Shredded Cucumber
	Lemon Juice & Zest
	Olive Oil
	Dill
	Salt & Pepper

Instructions

Cut chicken breast into cubes
Season with salt & pepper
Saute over medium high heat until edges start to crisp
Sprinkle with dill and add a splash of lemon juice and finish cooking 
Rough chop lettuce and tomato
Slice red onion into thin strips
In a bowl mix together greek yogurt with shredded cucumber and lemon juice & zest
Season with salt & pepper and add a drizzle of olive oil
Wrap all ingredients in pita bread and top with Tzatziki
