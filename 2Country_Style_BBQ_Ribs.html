Country Style BBQ Ribs

Ingredients

Country Style Pork Ribs
Oil
Dry Rub
	Paprika
	Cumin
	Onion Powder
	Garlic Powder
	Chili Powder
	Cayenne Pepper
	Oregano
	Black Pepper
	Salt
BBQ Sauce

Instructions

Slowcook Style:
Preheat oven to 300
Pat pork dry
Slice into thin strips widthwise
Generously coat with dry rub mixture
Grease a tin foil coated baking sheet
Place ribs in a single layer on tray
Cover tightly with foil
Bake for 2 to 2.5 hours
Remove the top layer of foil
Baste ribs with BBQ sauce and cook for 10 minutes
Flip, baste again, and cook for additional 10 minutes


Quick Way:
Preheat oven to 425
Pat pork dry
Slice into thin strips widthwise
Generously coat with dry rub mixture
Grease a tin foil coated baking sheet
Place ribs in a single layer on tray
Bake for 30 minutes
Baste ribs with BBQ sauce and cook for 10 minutes
Flip, baste again, and cook for additional 10 minutes
