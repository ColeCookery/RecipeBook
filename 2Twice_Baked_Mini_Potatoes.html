Twice Baked Mini Potatoes

Ingredients

Baby Potatoes
Oil
Shredded Cheese
Butter
Milk
Bacon Crumbles
Chives or Scallions
Salt & Pepper

Instructions

Preheat oven to 450
Place 2 tablespoons of butter into a bowl
Rinse potatoes and pierce each with a fork 
Microwave in a single layer for 4 minutes
Halve each potato and scoop out soft middle into the bowl
Lay out potato skins cut side down on a baking sheet
Coat with oil and sprinkle with salt
Bake for 15 minutes
In the bowl, add a splash of milk and cheese and mix
Season with salt & pepper
Spoon or pipe mixture back into baked potato skins
Broil until tops look melty and crispy
Top with bacon crumbles & garnish
